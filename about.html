About

Start Your Tech Literacy Journey. It’ll blow your mind.

As technology evolves and shapes our world, tech literacy becomes increasingly important.
In essence, tech literacy is the passport to success in the digital age. It empowers individuals to harness the full potential of technology while navigating its challenges responsibly. As technology continues to evolve, cultivating tech literacy becomes increasingly crucial for individuals and society as a whole.

Our learning platform, "iCoder"

offers a unique opportunity to introduce kids to coding in a fun, safe, and engaging way, using a game they already love. The skills developed using our platform can help foster a life-long interest in computer science, paving the way for future tech innovators or simply helping a child gain a sense of stability, safety, and confidence.

Unlock Your Learning Potential Today! Are you ready to take control of your learning journey?

Look no further! iCoder is here to revolutionize the way you learn..
Learn at Your Own Pace: Say goodbye to rigid schedules! With "iCoder", you can learn whenever and wherever suits you best. Whether it's early morning or late at night, your learning journey adapts to your lifestyle.

How does Mission Services Benefit Kids? With free access to the iCoder, funded by Harsh Raj's teams.

Young learners are able to access engaging educational content. In today’s rapidly evolving digital world, computer science education is no longer a luxury, but a necessity for individual growth, social development, and economic advancement. Providing free computer science education to underserved and foster children is a crucial step in fostering a more equitable and inclusive society. It helps address systemic inequalities and bridge the digital divide that disproportionately impacts these vulnerable populations.
